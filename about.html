SKY AIR

Proposal

Sky Airport is distanced from Sydney’s most populated areas, but it is easily accessible by Sydney suburbs. Located in Sydney’s West, Sky Airport is designed to eliminate distraction to locals and provide an enjoyable environment for every user. Due to the overwhelming number of people using the airport, Sky Airport has 33-metre-high flying elevators, passing through the middle of the structure, which allow efficient transportation of travellers. Positioned at a high distance from the ground, the surrounding area of the airport can be used as a playing and relaxing green area, suitable for people of all ages.

Precedents

Sky Airport is inspired by ZhiYong Hong and XueTing Zhang "Airport Skyscraper" and Gerasimos Pavlidis' "GreenGru". The landing platform is replicating the Indian anaconda, further adding to the overall naturistic design.
